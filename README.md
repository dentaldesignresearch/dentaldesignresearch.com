# Dental Design Research

**Dental Design Research** is an independent research initiative focused on understanding workflows, tools, and collaboration in modern dental design and digital dentistry.

The project explores how dental clinics, laboratories, and CAD/CAM designers work today — identifying challenges, gaps, and opportunities for improvement through structured research and data analysis.

---

## Website

Official website:  
https://dentaldesignresearch.com

This site is hosted using **GitHub Pages** and serves as the public landing page for the research project.

---

## Research Focus

The research currently focuses on:

- Dental CAD/CAM workflows  
- Collaboration between clinics, labs, and designers  
- Communication challenges and handoff processes  
- Tools and software used in dental design  
- Operational and scalability pain points  

All research is **non-clinical** and does not involve patient data.

---

## Research Ethics & Privacy

- Participation in surveys and interviews is voluntary  
- No patient or protected health information (PHI) is collected  
- Responses are analyzed in aggregated and anonymized form  
- Data is used strictly for research and exploratory purposes  

More details are available on the website’s **Research Ethics** page.
