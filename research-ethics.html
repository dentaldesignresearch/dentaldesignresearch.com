Research Ethics

Our Commitment to Ethical Research

At Dental Design Research, we believe that advancing the field of digital dentistry requires more than just data—it requires a foundation of trust. We are committed to conducting our research responsibly, transparently, and with the utmost respect for every participant's privacy and contribution.

Voluntary Participation

  • check_circle You may choose whether or not to participate in any of our studies.
  • check_circle You may skip any question you do not wish to answer.
  • check_circle You may stop participating at any time without penalty or explanation.

Data Privacy & Anonymity

  • security We do not sell data to third parties.
  • security Responses are anonymized during the analysis process.
  • security Individual answers are never publicly attributed to specific persons or companies.
insights

Use of Collected Information

Data collected through our research is used exclusively to identify industry trends and develop insights that help improve dental CAD/STL workflows. Our findings are shared as aggregated summaries to help the community better understand technology needs.

shield_with_heart

No Patient or Clinical Data

We do not collect Protected Health Information (PHI) or specific clinical patient records. Our research focuses strictly on professional workflows, software utilization, and operational challenges.

verified_user

Transparency & Integrity

We are dedicated to the honest representation of our findings. We maintain clear communication about our research goals and ensure that our reports accurately reflect the feedback we receive from the dental community.
